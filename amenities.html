Park your RV or pitch you tent at one of our 75 spacious campsites. Once you've settled in, enjoy Blue Mountain Campground's many Amenities, we've thought of everything to make your stay memorable and fun.

On-Site Laundry Room

Our on-site laundry facilities are available to all campers. Enjoy the outdoors and GET DIRTY, we’ve got you covered.

Camp Store

You don’t have to run into town to find camping necessities – our on-site convenience store has ice, wood, camping supplies and tent/sleeping bag rentals. Gifts, too!

Playground

Your “li’l campers” will have hours of fun climbing and crawling on the fort, riding the slide, playing on the teeter-totters & swings and much more!
