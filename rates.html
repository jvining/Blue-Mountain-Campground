Blue Mountain Campground Policies

Check-in: Check-in must be by 10PM on Friday and Saturday and by 9PM on Sunday- Thursday.
Check-out: 11:00 AM
Payments: Payments are paid in advance for campsites. All campers, guests, and extra vehicles must be registered, identified and paid for on arrival. For your convenience we accept MasterCard, Visa, Discover & American Express.
Conduct: Guest stay is at the discretion of the park management. Quiet hours are from 11:00 PM until 8:30 AM and are strictly enforced. Exceptionally loud noises, radios, generators, etc. are undesirable at any time. This is a family oriented park. Profane or obscene conduct is strictly prohibited and will be cause for immediate eviction. Parents are responsible for the safety of their children at all times.
Pets: Must be kept on a leash at all times.  We do not accept breed/crossbreeds listed in the Dangerous Dogs Act.
Campfires: Use fire rings provided at campsite. Fires not permitted elsewhere.
Firearms & Fireworks: These items ARE NOT PERMITTED at any time under any circumstances.
Trees: Trees on the grounds contribute to the natural beauty of the Park. Do not deface or mutilate with axes, nails or knives.
Cancellations: Cancellations are accepted up to 48 hours prior to scheduled check-in time, except for cabins. No Cancellations/Refunds on weekends or holidays.
