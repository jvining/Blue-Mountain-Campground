Blue Mountain Campground Offers Premium RV Parks, Tent Sites and Cabins in Ulster County, NY.

The Catskill Mountains of New York were made for camping enjoyment and Blue Mountain Campground offers quality facilities and easy access to area attractions in Saugerties, Woodstock, Hunter and all of Ulster and Greene counties.

We’re open mid-April through mid-October for Seasonals and year-round for Full Hook-up Rigs. Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover accepted.
